About Us

Bluechip99 MANAGEMENT LLC is a vanguard artist and musician management company based in NYC. We specialize in representing innovative and diverse artists, primarily from the queer community.

Our mission is to support and promote artists who are pushing boundaries and creating new standards in the music industry. We provide comprehensive management services including touring, merchandising, and social media promotion.

With a focus on inclusivity and diversity, we are committed to giving a platform to underrepresented voices in the music world. Join us in celebrating and supporting the vibrant and dynamic work of our artists.
